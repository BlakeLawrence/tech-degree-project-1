The Things I Love.

What makes me, me!

Technology

It goes without say that I am a lover of all things tech.

I have always been a sucker for gadgets and technology is what drives some of the things I love most.

The Outdoors

Coming from South Africa, I was lucky enough to grow up in a city where great weather was a year round treat.

I grew up camping, fishing, hiking and exploring what nature had on offer.

I will always posses a strong love for the outdoors.

Music

I fell in love with guitars at an early age when I stumbled across my fathers Jimi Hendrix record, I put it on and the rest was history.

I play guitar and drums (to a very modest level unfortunately) and I love listening to all kinds of music.

Sports

Since I can remember, I have always been a keen sportsman.

I grew up as a kid playing football at a local club and was always naturally a strong swimmer.

When I went to high school, I played Rugby, Cricket, Water Polo and I participated in Athletics. I also Love most ocean sports and two of my favourites are surfing and deep sea kayak fishing.
